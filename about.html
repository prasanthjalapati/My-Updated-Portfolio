About

I’m Prasanth Jalapati, a Data Analyst with over 5 years of experience in healthcare and finance domains, currently based in Kansas, USA. My work bridges data engineering, advanced analytics, and regulatory compliance to drive impactful, data-informed decisions. I specialize in building scalable ETL pipelines using tools like Apache NiFi, SSIS, and Azure Data Factory, and I’ve developed machine learning models for critical use cases such as patient readmission prediction, financial fraud detection, and chronic disease risk analysis. With a strong command of Python (Scikit-learn, XGBoost), SQL, and cloud platforms like AWS and Azure, I bring end-to-end data solutions to life—from ingestion and modeling to visualization and compliance. My dashboards, built in Tableau and Power BI, have empowered healthcare providers and financial institutions with real-time insights, while my NLP expertise using SpaCy and BERT has enhanced both clinical and anti-money laundering applications. I’m passionate about transforming raw data into actionable intelligence that not only improves business performance but also aligns with industry standards such as HIPAA, GDPR, PCI-DSS, and RBI regulations. Let’s connect if you’re looking for someone who thrives at the intersection of data, technology, and compliance.
